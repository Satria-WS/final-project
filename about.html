About Us

sukalapak's mission is to democratize commerce through technology. Since its founding in 2009, Sukalapak has been a force that pioneers digital transformation in Indonesia. Our vision is to build an ecosystem where anyone can start and discover anything. We empower millions of merchants and users across our marketplace, logistics, payments, and financial technology businesses. Headquartered in Jakarta, we are building a world-class technology company that creates opportunities, increase financial inclusion and help people achieve more. Join our movement.
